About Me

Praman White is a driven professional with a diverse background in technology, logistics, and application development. A proud member of the Greater Seattle Area since 2002, Praman has developed a passion for innovation and efficiency in every role he has taken on. With extensive experience in retail logistics and customer service, Praman excelled as an E-Commerce Manager at Kroger and an E-Commerce Logistics Supervisor at Fred Meyer, where he led teams to optimize online order fulfillment processes and improve customer satisfaction. His expertise in workflow management and process improvements has earned him recognition for his leadership and operational skills. Praman is also deeply committed to advancing his technical abilities. He is currently enrolled in the Year Up United program, specializing in application development, where he is expanding his knowledge of programming and software solutions. A community-oriented individual, Praman has a strong history of volunteer work, including over two years spent assisting special needs students, where he fostered inclusivity and understanding through personalized support. In his personal life, Praman is a forward-thinking polymath who enjoys developing innovative ideas and solutions that positively impact his workplace and community. He is committed to his goal of driving technological advancement and making meaningful contributions to his field. When he's not working or learning, Praman cherishes time with his loved ones, including his partner, Jasper, with whom he dreams of building a beautiful future together. Whether leading a team, solving technical challenges, or creating new solutions, Praman White is dedicated to excellence and continuous growth in all that he does.
